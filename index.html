About Us

Welcome to Swati Secure Systems..

Swati Secure Systems is committed to provide complete one point security to you vide CCTV & Surveillance system, Fire Safety system, PA system and A/V system integrator, based in Pune, India. We provide comprehensive system design, installation, commissioning and after-sales support services for projects as per with international standards and within your budget. VICOM’s R&D team surveys the industry environment, variety of customer needs and come out with latest products & technologies and provide state-of-the-art solutions. Customer protection and satisfaction is highly important to us. Even a small lapse may have potentially dangerous outcome. We partner with each customer to understand their specific security needs, and then design a system directly around those needs, and establish a long-term relationship. Whether you need an Access Control or Surveillance System, you can trust that we will install a system with your safety in mind.
